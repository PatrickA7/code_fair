ABOUT US

The CDU IT Code Fair was created to give a platform for talented university students who are aspiring designers, creators, innovators, and artists to showcase their work. Ever since it started in insert date the growth of the event has has been enormous to get the sponsorship of the NT Government and major IT companies in Australia such as the Australia Computer Society, NEC, Kinetic IT and more. For 2017 we hope the event will be bigger than ever and we hope to the creations that people up with!

PRIZES

The prizes that you can get depend on the type of competition you enter in. Each competition has several awards for the different type of participants. The Award categories as of now are Academic Awards, Business Innovation Challenge, Innovation Award, Poster Competition, Best Novice Coder, Best Intermediate Coder, Best Advance Coder, and the People’s Choice Awards.

You can also win jobs! The NT Government will employ students to turn their ideas into reality!

LOCATION

The location of the event will be at the Charles Darwin University’s North Australian Centre for Oil and Gas (Building Pink 7).

Like every competition there are some rules but unlike them we only have one! That rule is that you have to be a university student and that includes being a VET student.
