PRIZES

The prizes that you can get depend on the type of competition you enter in. Each competition has several awards for the different type of participants. The Award categories as of now are Academic Awards, Business Innovation Challenge, Innovation Award, Poster Competition, Best Novice Coder, Best Intermediate Coder, Best Advance Coder, and the People’s Choice Awards. You can also win jobs! The NT Government will employ students to turn their ideas into reality! “VIEW ALL THE PRIZES”
